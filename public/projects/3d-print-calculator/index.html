3D Print Cost Calculator
How To Use:
1. Fill in your print details using the fields provided in the calculator.
2. Check your slicer software (e.g., Ultimaker Cura) for extra details like filament used (grams) and exact print time to ensure accuracy.
3. Decide how much you want to charge by setting your hourly labor rate and Profit Margin (%).
4. Print the summary for a complete and easy-to-reference record of your costs and final price.
